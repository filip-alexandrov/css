APY during the first 2 Weeks

To ensure the security of our protocol, there is 2 Weeks 0% vesting period, which you wont receive any interest on your staked asset. After the 2 Week period is over, you will be automatically quallified for receiving interest and the interest will be automatically reinvested. You can withdraw the invested amount at any time, but be aware that such action will result in restarting the 2 Week 0% vesting period.
